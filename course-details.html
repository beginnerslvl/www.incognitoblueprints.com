⚠ DISCLAIMER

It's not only about the Harvards & the MITs. As much as everyone wants to aim for the most prestigious universities in USA they are still hard to reach for a majority of people & not getting into them isn't the end of the world. That doesn't mean you don't give your best! I have taken multiple rejections in my life as a student & my early career life, so I cannot stress enough, how important it is for everyone take it easy, and not be so hard on yourself. Aim big, give it your best, because the end goal isn't the Ivy, its to learn & grow to be a better version of yourself. 🚀

I have structured this course to suit to the needs of anyone who is applying to US-based colleges regardless of being in the Ivy League. The name comes off as Ivy League because I want you to prepare a profile that is Ivy League worthy and aim for the best.
