To be completely transparent with you… 🧼

  • Students are not guaranteed a seat in the group mentorship program if they pay the application fee

  • Students who apply, may be asked for additional documentation for verification

  • Application fee will not be refunded for students that are not selected

  • Application fee will be refunded for students that are selected, for example

    • Students that are selected will pay the enrollment fee of 64,700 INR if the application fee was 200 INR

  • Application fee waivers are currently not available for any students

Who is the group mentorship for?

✔ Prospective undergraduate students only

✔ Applying for the Spring or Fall 2024 intake to universities in the USA

✔ Currently entered class 12 or completed class 12 (gap year student)

✔ Obtaining passing marks in all school subjects and in good academic standing

✔ Able to spend time, complete assignments & work on college application (approx. 4-6 hours/week)

✔ Serious about applying to colleges in USA, not as backup

💡 What’s in it for you?

  • ✍🏼 Essay Review

    One essay review by your Ivy League mentor with personalized feedback and structural improvements.

  • 📓 Ivy League 101 Course

    Automatic access to the Ivy League 101 course after the mentorship concludes. Scheduled to launch Sep-Oct 2023.

  • 📝 College Application Fee Waiver

    A template and crash course on all the different methods to successfully secure a waiver and apply to US colleges for free.

  • 🎧 TOEFL Discount

    A discount code allowing students to receive $20 off one of the most widely accepted English Proficiency test TOEFL.

  • 📔 Resume Template

    Up to 5 formats of editable resume templates that are suggested by Ivy League colleges in the USA for students.

  • 🚀 College List

    Live workshop to shortlist colleges and an excel spreadsheet template to help students build their final college list.

  • 💰 CSS Profile Map

    Excel spreadsheet template that can be used to compile finances for students and their families to correctly fill out the CSS profile and obtain the maximum financial aid.

  • 🧱 One Month Mentorship

    One month of mentorship under the guidance of an experienced Ivy League mentor with 5 live sessions and Q&A workshops.

  • 📞 24x7 Offline Support

    Access to an exclusive Slack community with your team members and Ivy League mentor to answer your doubts anytime 24x7 during your mentorship.

🧪Group Mentorship FAQs

  • The application fee supports our team who reviews the applications to shortlist who will be selected.

  • Yes- students that are selected for the group mentorship will receive a refund of 200 INR and need to pay 69,800 INR as a remaining balance to enroll in the group mentorship program.

  • At this time we are not offering installment based payment methods. The entire fee will need to be paid upfront.

  • At this time we are not offering any scholarships or discounts for the road to success mentorship program.

  • Students will have access to the Slack groups from 30 Jul - 31 Aug 2023.

  • Since this is a group mentorship, all the 1-1 meetings will be grouped and include the mentor plus 6 students of the cohort. No student will have individual meetings with the mentor. If you are interested in exclusive 1-1 mentorships, you can explore our individual mentorship programs here.

  • This group mentorship is ideal for students applying for the Spring or Fall 2024 intake as they can work on their college applications during the mentorship. If you are not applying for either of these intakes, this mentorship could still be beneficial to you as you will be able to prepare for your college applications with your mentor before your college applications open.

  • All live sessions will be scheduled on the weekends. The first live session will occur on 30 Jul 2023 at 11 AM EST (8:30 PM IST). During the 1st live session mentorship, students and the mentor will mutually decide on a time suitable for everyone and the next 3 meetings will be scheduled accordingly.

  • At this time we have not opened enrollment for cohort #2.

Fine Print

Does this course include one-on-one live sessions with a mentor?

Unfortunately no. The live sessions are grouped, however if you are looking for one-on-one assistance, feel free to check out any one of our mentorship programs as ALL of them are one-on-one.

I didn’t read the details and applied by mistake, since I am not eligible to join the mentorship, can I get a refund on the application fee?

There will be no refunds provided under any circumstances.

Can I get a refund if I am not satisfied with the group mentorship?

Unfortunately, at this point because of how the mentorship is structured for a small group, we cannot offer refunds.

Since the price is in Indian rupees, do I need to have an Indian card to purchase?

You can pay using any credit card across our website. Your bank will automatically convert to the necessary currency.

I want to use another payment option or my payment option is not working, who do I contact?

Please reach out to us at info@incognitoblueprints.com for exploring payment options like UPI, GooglePay, Paytm, etc.
