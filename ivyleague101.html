Fine Print

When will I get access to the course?

Students will get full access to the course immediately upon purchase through their custom student portals.

How long will I have access to the course?

Students will have unlimited access to the course till 30 June 2024.

I am not applying for this admission cycle (Fall 2024), is this course for me?

Absolutely. This course is designed for students who are interested in knowing the A-Z of applying to study in the USA as an international student. Even if you are not applying this season, completing this course will allow you to be better prepared for when you do.

How do the live sessions work?

All students who enroll before 06Aug2023 will be able to attend 2 Live sessions with Saloni as part of the course.

Students that enroll after these dates will have access to the Live session via recordings.

Does this course include one-on-one live sessions with a mentor?

Unfortunately no. The live sessions are grouped, however if you are looking for one-on-one assistance, feel free to check out any one of our mentorship programs as ALL of them are one-on-one.

Can I get a refund if I am not satisfied with the course?

Unfortunately, at this point because of how detailed the course is structured, we cannot offer any refunds.

Since the price is in Indian rupees, do I need to have an Indian card to purchase?

You can pay using any credit card across our website. Your bank will automatically convert to the necessary currency.

I want to use another payment option or my payment option is not working, who do I contact?

Please reach out to us at info@incognitoblueprints.com for exploring payment options like UPI, GooglePay, Paytm, etc.
